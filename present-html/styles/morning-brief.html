Analysis Opinion

"The best interface is no interface."

-- Golden Krishna, Author

We've spent decades making screens brighter, sharper, and more addictive. The next revolution isn't about a better screen — it's about no screen at all.

The companies that win won't be the ones with the flashiest displays. They'll be the ones whose technology you never notice — until it's gone.

Key takeaway: Design for absence, not presence. The interface of the future is the world itself.
