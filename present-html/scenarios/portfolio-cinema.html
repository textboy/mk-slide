Methodology

How I Work

Every project is different, but the principles stay the same.

  • 01 Listen before sketching. The first two weeks are conversations, not wireframes. I map stakeholder assumptions against user reality.
  • 02 Design in systems, not screens. Components, tokens, and patterns come before pixels. This is how you scale without losing coherence.
  • 03 Prototype the risk. I build interactive prototypes of the hardest interaction first — the part where most projects stall.
  • 04 Ship, measure, refine. Design doesn't end at handoff. I stay involved through launch and track outcomes against the original hypothesis.
  • 05 Document the decisions. Future teams inherit your thinking, not just your files. Every major decision gets a written rationale.
