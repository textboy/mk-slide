Process
Methodology

How I Build Brand Motion

  • 01 Brand Audit. I deconstruct the existing identity — what works, what's broken, what's missing. Every element gets stress-tested across contexts.
  • 02 Motion DNA. Before any animation, I define the brand's kinetic personality: speed curves, rhythm patterns, spatial behaviors.
  • 03 System Build. I create the full toolkit — logo variants, color motion rules, type animations, social templates, and transition libraries.
  • 04 Handoff + Train. Lottie files, After Effects templates, Figma components. Your team can execute without me in the room.
