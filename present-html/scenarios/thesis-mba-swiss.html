01 / MOTIVATION

Research Question

How does hybrid/remote work reshape organizational culture, and what moderating factors determine whether culture strengthens or erodes?

  • ● 74% of Fortune 500 adopted permanent hybrid models post-2022
  • ● Cultural alignment scores dropped 18 points on average (Gallup Q12)
  • ● CEOs cite “culture erosion” as #1 remote work concern (McKinsey, 2025)

* Gallup, "State of the Global Workplace 2025"; McKinsey, "The Organization Blog", March 2025

02 / METHOD

Mixed-Methods Design

Quantitative

  • N = 2,847 (42 firms)
  • 63-item Likert survey
  • Hierarchical regression + SEM
  • Response rate: 68.3%

Qualitative

  • 36 semi-structured interviews
  • Balanced role representation
  • Thematic analysis
  • κ = 0.84

04 / IMPLICATIONS

Practical Implications

01

Formalize Leader Communication

Daily async updates are the strongest culture predictor (β = 0.41).

02

Redesign Hybrid Onboarding

New hires show 2.1× higher cultural dissonance. Immersion weeks offset this.

03

Measure Culture Continuously

Quarterly OCI pulse checks outperform annual engagement surveys.
